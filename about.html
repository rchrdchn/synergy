Synergy Massage & Wellness is committed to fostering balance, wellness and productivity in workplaces throughout the Bay Area. Through our quality-focused, customizable mobile onsite chair massage programs, we provide employers an affordable way to increase the well-being and satisfaction of their most important asset: their employees.

Linda

Linda Mach

CAMTC #17096

Linda brings compassion, positive energy and balance into her clients' lives through her massage and bodywork. After over a decade working for youth development non-profit organizations, Linda started Synergy Massage & Wellness in 2011, as a small private practice out of her home studio in the Mission area of San Francisco.

While Linda still heals through her private practice, she is now focused on the mobile side of the business: her customizable on-site chair massage programs. Through on-site chair massage programs, she is able to introduce more people in the Bay Area to the healing benefits of massage, self-care and a regular wellness practice.

A practicing yogi and wellness enthusiast, Linda understands the importance of the mind-body connection and experiencing life through the senses – unplugged and in real time. Through her community and her work, Linda embraces a life lived deeply with intention, integrity, and a passion for living.

Catherine

Catherine

CAMTC #24388

After 10 years of working in child and family services, Catherine moved from the east coast to California to study massage. She transformed her passion for helping others to a passion for bodywork.

Her style is a therapeutic massage based on the various modalities that she has studied and her understanding of body assessment mixed together to meet clients individual needs. Her motivation is for each client to walk away from the massage feeling relaxed, balanced and rejuvenated.
